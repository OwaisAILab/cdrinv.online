How to Register & Login

A complete step-by-step guide to getting started with the CDR Intelligence Portal — from payment verification to your first login.

Platform: cdrinv.online Access: Restricted / Authorized Personnel Category: Getting Started
CDRInv.Online is a restricted-access investigation platform designed for authorized officers. To maintain security and integrity, every account must go through a verified registration and approval process. This guide walks you through each step — from selecting a subscription plan to logging in for the first time.

Step 01

01

Subscribe to a Plan

Before you can register, you need to select a subscription plan and complete payment. The platform offers three tiers designed to match different operational requirements and durations.

Basic

1 Month · 30 days of platform access

Single CDR analysis only · Up to 30 CDRs

Standard

6 Months · 180 days of platform access

Single & Dual CDR analysis · Unlimited CDRs

Extended

1 Year · 365 days of platform access

All features · Geo-Fence record search · Unlimited CDRs

💳 Payment Details: Make your payment to PK44MEZN0099110107413195 Account Title Shaikh Abdullah— Meezan Bank Ltd. Keep the Transaction ID safe; you will need it during registration.

Step 02

02

Submit Registration

Click the Register button on the homepage and complete the registration form with your details.

Registration fields:

  • Username — Choose a unique identifier (e.g., officer_id)
  • Email Address — Must be valid for OTP delivery
  • Password — At least 8 chars with uppercase, lowercase, number, and special character
  • Transaction ID — The payment reference from Step 01
  • Subscription Plan — Select the plan you paid for

🔒 Password Requirements: Your password must contain at least one uppercase letter (A-Z), one lowercase letter (a-z), one number (0-9), and one special character (!@#$%^&*). The built-in strength meter will guide you.

Step 03

03

Verify Your Email

After submitting your registration, you will be redirected to the OTP verification page. A 6-digit OTP will be sent to your registered email address.

📧

Check Your Inbox

The OTP arrives within seconds. Check your spam folder if you don't see it.

⏱️

10-Minute Expiry

The OTP expires after 10 minutes. Click Resend if it expires.

✅

Enter the Code

Enter the 6-digit code to verify your identity and proceed.

⏳ Didn't receive the OTP? Click the Resend button. If the issue persists, check your spam folder or contact the administrator.

Step 04

04

Await Admin Approval

Once your email is verified, your account status changes to Pending Approval. An administrator will review your payment and verify your transaction ID.

What happens next:

  • Administrator verifies your payment
  • Typically takes up to 30 minutes during business hours
  • You receive an email notification on approval
  • The pending page auto-checks for status updates

🔄 Auto-check enabled: You don't need to refresh the page manually — it checks for status updates automatically every 10 seconds.

Step 05

05

Login with OTP

Once your account is approved, you can log in to the platform. Every login requires a fresh OTP for security.

1. Enter Credentials

Enter your registered email and password on the login page.

2. Receive OTP

A 6-digit OTP is sent to your email. Enter it to complete the login.

🔐 Security-first: Every login requires a fresh OTP — ensuring your account is always protected against unauthorized access.

Troubleshooting

Common Issues & Fixes

🔑 Forgot Password?
Click the Forgot password? link on the login page. Enter your registered email, and a reset OTP will be sent. Follow the instructions to set a new password.

📧 Didn't Receive OTP?
Check your spam/junk folder. If you still haven't received it, click the Resend button. If the issue persists, contact the administrator.

⚠️ Account Rejected?
Your registration may be rejected if the transaction ID is incorrect or the payment cannot be verified. Double-check your Transaction ID and re-apply, or contact the administrator for assistance.
