It helps investigators identify communication patterns, suspect networks, call frequency, and location trails. CDR Intelligence Overview

CDR analysis software processes Call Detail Records generated by telecom operators. It helps investigators identify communication patterns, detect networks, track locations, and analyze call behavior for intelligence and forensic investigations.
